Proton Mail

You can contact me securely and anonymously through Proton Mail, a secure e-mail service. Proton Mail provides adequate security in many cases. Proton has a blog post about its threat model.

Preferably, please use the Tor browser to create an anonymous Proton Mail address. hen send your message to: daanvanmonsjou@protonmail.com via your new Proton Mail address.

Tip: Proton Mail has a a separate URL for use with the Tor browser. You cannot open this URL with a regular web browser, like Firefox or Chrome.
