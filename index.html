About Me

I'm a technology journalist at Tweakers, where I write stories about semiconductors and PC hardware. Previously, I did two internships and freelance work at Tweakers. Before that, I wrote for British computer website OC3D, among others.

I've written stories about the technological strides at TSMC, I graduated on the EU's chip ambitions, I covered the situation at Intel and I interviewed ASML's CTO, Martin van den Brink.
